CONTROLS

  • W / S — drive forward / reverse   A / D — steer
  • Mouse — aim turret   Left click / Space — fire main gun
  • F — coaxial machine gun (hold)   G — deploy a smoke screen
  • Q — call an artillery barrage   E — call an air strike, at the mouse point (if available; danger close!)
  • Tab — take command of the next friendly tank (Shift+Tab previous)
  • Right-drag — orbit camera   Middle-drag / arrows — pan   Wheel — zoom
  • Cross rivers only at bridges · shells punch through cover · disabled crews bail out and fight on
  • Your gun can hit anything you aim at — structures, terrain, and tanks/crew of either side (friendly fire is on)
